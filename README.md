Data Visualization Python project using the Altair library to visualize a 25 year history of offensive football statstics in the NFL. The investigation aimed to correlate a general rise in offensive production over the last 25 years due partly to rule changes that favored offensive players by increasing defensive penalites. The modifications of NFL rules made it increasingly difficult for a defensive player guarding a pass catcher (WR,RB,TE) as seen by a rise in defensive holding and pass interference penalties. Line graphs are used to show continuous changes over time with respect to total yards, passing yards, and rushing yards. Scatter plots are utilized to show potential correlation between Penalty Yards vs Total Yards and Penalty Yards vs Passing yards. This the final project for CSCA 5702-Fundamentals of Data Visualization course at the University of Colorado Boulder as part of the Masters in Computer Science program. Can we visualize and correlate an increase in penalties and offensive production simultaneously?
